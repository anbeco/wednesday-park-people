What to bring

Everyone brings

  • A potluck dish for 6-8 people
  • Camp chairs or a blanket
  • Plates and utensils for your crew
  • Drinks for your family

Costumes are encouraged and never required. Kids and dogs welcome.

Annie brings every time

  • Costco pizza as an anchor
  • Guacamole and chips (vegan-friendly)
  • Gloves for stream cleanup on Jul 15

Come to one, come to all seven, or anything in between. Summer is busy and partial attendance is the whole point.
